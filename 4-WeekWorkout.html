Holistic General Fitness

True physical fitness goes beyond just lifting weights or running. This project focuses on the core pillars of physical health: Cardiovascular Endurance, Muscular Strength, Flexibility, and Body Composition, fueled by a balanced Filipino diet.

💪 Muscular Strength & Endurance

Bodyweight and light resistance exercises designed to build lean muscle mass, increase bone density, and improve daily functional movements.

🫀 Cardiovascular Health

A mix of Low-Intensity Steady State (LISS) and interval training to strengthen the heart, improve lung capacity, and boost stamina.

🧘 Flexibility & Mobility

Dynamic and static stretching routines incorporated to improve joint range of motion, prevent injuries, and correct posture.

🍚 Performance Nutrition

Utilizing macro-balanced, local Filipino ingredients to provide sustained energy for workouts and the necessary proteins for muscle recovery.

Recovery & Lifestyle Tips

You don't get stronger while working out; you get stronger while recovering. Optimize your downtime.

💧 Optimal Hydration

  • Drink 3-4 liters daily. Muscles are 75% water. Dehydration leads to cramps and decreased strength output.
  • Pre-Workout: Drink 500ml of water 2 hours before exercising.
  • Post-Workout: Weigh yourself before and after sweaty cardio sessions; drink water to replace the lost weight.

🌙 Sleep & Muscle Growth

  • 7–9 hours of sleep. The majority of Human Growth Hormone (HGH) is released during deep sleep, which rebuilds muscles.
  • Protein before bed. A light protein snack (like a boiled egg) provides amino acids while you sleep.
  • Active Recovery. Don't sit all day on rest days. Light walking helps flush lactic acid from sore muscles.
